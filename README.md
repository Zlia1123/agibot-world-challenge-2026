# AGIBOT World Challenge @ ICRA 2026

An embodied AI benchmark competition focusing on **reasoning-action loops and world models for robotics**.

---

## 🚀 Overview

The **AGIBOT World Challenge** is an international robotics competition associated with **ICRA 2026 (IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation)**.

The goal is to advance **embodied intelligence** by evaluating how robotic systems integrate:

- perception
- reasoning
- planning
- action

---

## 🧠 Competition Tracks

### 1. Reasoning–Action Track
Focus on integrating perception, reasoning, and decision-making in embodied agents.

### 2. World Model Track
Focus on predictive modeling of environment dynamics and robot interaction.
