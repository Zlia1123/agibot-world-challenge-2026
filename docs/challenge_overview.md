# Challenge Overview

The AGIBOT World Challenge @ ICRA 2026 is an embodied AI benchmark competition for robotics.

## Tracks

### 1. Reasoning–Action Track
This track focuses on integrating perception, reasoning, planning, and action in embodied robotic systems.

### 2. World Model Track
This track focuses on predictive modeling of environment dynamics and robot interaction.

## Goals

The challenge aims to support research and engineering efforts in:

- embodied AI
- robot learning
- world models
- sim-to-real transfer
- decision-making for robotic systems

## Finals

The finals will take place at **ICRA 2026 in Vienna** using **real robots**.
